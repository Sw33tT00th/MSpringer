Homepage

This site is essentially a test. As a budding web developer the whole point of this site is to give a first attempt at creating a custom theme and/or plugins. It is also here for me to learn how to actually get a domain name as well as some actual webspace.

I also intend to use this site as an actual blog (partly to see if I have anything resembling skill in writing) with three categories

  • Lynda Logs – A blog about going through some playlists on lynda.com starting with one on WordPress development.
  • Reader’s Indigestion – A blog concerning books, some that I liked, some that I didn’t, and my various moody opinions on them.
  • Gamer’s Gluttony – A blog focused on what games I’ve been playing lately and what I think of them.

I have no idea how consistent with writing anything on this, but here the site is just the same.
